# TWIN — The Badge System (designed to be collected like Discord)
*Deep research: 6 agents, badge psychology + Discord/Xbox/PlayStation/Steam + art direction + scarcity economy. This replaces the old generic prompts — it's a SYSTEM, not six images.*

---

## ★ THE 10 RULES THAT MAKE BADGES WANTED

1. **EARN, NEVER BUY.** No badge is purchasable. Money can unlock *access to a behavior* (Pro), never the achievement. Sell frames/skins/showcase slots — never the badge. The moment a status badge can be bought, it signals wealth not merit and devalues every other badge.
2. **SCARCITY IS THE VALUE — AND IT MUST BE VISIBLE.** Every badge shows its live earned-% ("held by 3.4% of TWINs"). Flagship badges stay under ~25-30% (Yu-kai Chou's Rarity Test). A badge everyone has is wallpaper.
3. **NO PARTICIPATION TROPHIES** (the #1 killer). Never a badge for signup/profile-completion/one tap — the 100th trivial badge retroactively cheapens the first 99. Every badge must pass: **Share Test** (mention it unprompted?), **Rarity Test** (<30%?), **Story Test** (explain it without seeing the art?).
4. **A BADGE IS A MEMORIAL THAT DOES SOMETHING.** Foursquare died because badges did nothing. TWIN badges unlock real utility: AXON depth, extra streak-freezes, feed-tuning, showcase priority, themes. The badge is a *key*, not decoration.
5. **TIERED LADDERS, NOT ONE-AND-DONE.** Core families escalate (streak 7/30/100/365) so one badge motivates across the whole lifecycle, with a capstone that becomes the screenshot flag.
6. **LOSS AVERSION OUT-PULLS GAIN (~2×).** Signature consistency badges are *losable* and decay — but always with a forgiving streak-freeze so it motivates without dread.
7. **NEVER START A PROGRESS BAR AT 0%.** Pre-credit momentum ("you're 2 of 7 toward Insight Master") + show collection state ("9 of 14 collected"). A cosmetic reframe lifts completion ~15 points.
8. **PUBLIC + COMMUNITY-SCOPED = STATUS.** Badges live on profiles, posts, comments — never buried in settings. Zone-specific badges (Wealth/Mind/Health/Mission) signal *belonging*, a stronger motivator than generic completion.
9. **SURPRISE + STORY MAKE THEM SHAREABLE.** Mix predictable progression with hidden/secret badges the twin detects from real behavior. Unexpected unlocks are what people brag about.
10. **SYSTEM-ASSIGNED ORDER (non-reorderable).** Like Discord, TWIN decides display order by rarity. A rare badge always announces itself in a spot you can't fake.

## ★ THE BADGE SHAPE — "The Ascension Keystone" (the locked chassis, NOT a coin)

Every badge shares ONE silhouette: an **upward keystone/wedge** — narrow flat base, walls flaring out to broad square shoulders, with a **double-chevron »» notch carved into the crown** and a **thin vertical platinum seam** splitting it into two mirrored halves (the seam widens toward the top so the negative-space channel reads as a rising arrow).

**Why it wins:** encodes the whole thesis in the outline — keystone = the sovereign/locked self, upward flare = ascension, center seam = twin duality (current self | better self), crown »» = the logo, hidden rising arrow = the FedEx "aha." Trademark-defensible (nobody in self-improvement owns it), reads as solid black at 24px, and the broad upper field is a clean stage for the glyph.

**Rules:** the silhouette is IDENTICAL at every tier/family — only material, frame, core, and surface treatment change (never deform the body). Outline-level rank cue: **how many of the 3 crown chevrons are lit** (1→2→3) signals tier even in pure silhouette.
- **Tiers** escalate by material: Steel (gunmetal, 1 chevron) → Platinum (2) → **Champagne gold (3, the hero look)** → Gem-on-obsidian (gem core glowing through the seam) → **liquid-platinum Genesis** (molten, seam = live rivulet, animated).
- **Families** change surface only: **Team** = glyph+seam engraved INTO the stone (struck-seal authority); **Subscriber** = a live crystal core glowing up through the seam ("breathing"); **Gifter** = whole keystone shifts to rose-gold + gift-sparks at the crown.
- **Special accent shape:** the faceted *Ascending Gem* (teardrop→»» apex) is reserved as a top-tier "evolved" graduation form, used sparingly.

**Sample art prompt (Verified, champagne tier) — use this contour for every badge:**
> *Premium collectible profile badge for TWIN. Silhouette = a single upward-pointing KEYSTONE (narrow flat base, walls flaring to broad square shoulders); the crown/top edge is carved with a crisp double-chevron »» notch (two nested V-grooves, points up); a thin polished platinum SEAM runs vertically dead-center base-to-crown, splitting it into two mirrored halves, widening toward the top so the gap reads as a rising arrow. Material = champagne gold body, slim platinum bevel frame hugging the walls, all three crown chevrons lit, soft inner glow, deep obsidian-black background, subtle specular + faint gold rim light. Center glyph = a bold chunky checkmark in the broad upper field astride the seam, uncropped, high contrast. Luxury minimal emblem, single solid recognizable contour, embossed metal, no gradients-as-crutch, reads as a solid black silhouette at 24px (thick strokes, »» notch + seam visible at tiny scale). Front-on, centered, square canvas, champagne gold + platinum + obsidian only.*
> Swap only the **center glyph** + **tier material** for every other badge; keep the keystone, seam, and crown »» constant.

## ★ ART-DIRECTION RULEBOOK (this is what stops them looking cheap)

- **ONE BADGE = shared chassis + tier material + unique inner glyph.** Lock the chassis identically across the whole set (same ring geometry, same top-left 45° light, same bevel/specular). Only the **glyph + tier material/frame** change. This "milled from one factory" consistency is what creates the *complete-the-set* compulsion. (No six-identical-orbs; every glyph must be unmistakably distinct.)
- **SILHOUETTE-FIRST, 24px is the true size.** Must read as a solid black shape at 24px. One bold focal symbol, thick strokes, no fine texture, no gradients-as-information. Design at 512px, then QA at 24px AND as a grayscale silhouette.
- **NEVER rely on color alone for tier** (colorblind/grayscale safe): each tier has a distinct *frame* (plain ring → gold-seam → notched → laurel → ornate jeweled → liquid corona).
- **One clear metaphor per badge** (flame = streak, prism = insight, iris+check = verified, seed = founder). Meaning survives without text.
- **Gem inlays only at Epic+.** Max 4-6 colors per badge.
- **Blackout skin** (copying Apple Activity Awards): an all-platinum monochrome variant of the set, unlockable as a Pro/prestige flex + dark-mode look.
- **Append to every art prompt:** *"premium collectible minted-coin/enamel-pin badge, [tier material], central glyph [X], [tier frame], top-left 45° light, beveled, controlled specular, embossed, deep obsidian-black field, NO gradient background, NO texture noise, reads as a solid silhouette at 24px, champagne-gold + platinum luxury brand, [N] colors max, 512px."*

## ★ THREE SPECIAL FAMILIES: Team · Subscriber · Gifter (deep-researched design)

**The system (how they fit yet stay distinct):**
- **Shared chassis, different MINT.** All three reuse the exact earned-merit coin geometry (same ring, 45° top-left light, bevel, 24px-legible, 4-6 colors) — so they sit in a row as siblings. Only the **material + glyph + frame** change, never the geometry.
- **The Anti-Merit Rule (one-glance separation).** Merit climbs the metal ladder (steel→platinum→gold→gem). The three families are FORBIDDEN that ladder; each owns ONE signature material merit never uses: **Team = struck obsidian + platinum SEAL (intaglio/engraved-IN, not relief); Subscriber = a LIVE charged CRYSTAL core; Gifter = ROSE-GOLD (a hue found nowhere else).** Even at 24px, material alone says "not an achievement."
- **Glyph grammar is segregated.** Merit = accomplishment symbols (laurels/peaks/stars). Team = **house crests** (only the issuer can mint). Subscriber = **one evolving crystal**. Gifter = **warm giving glyphs** (gift/cupped hands/giving-ring). No crown/sword/laurel ever enters Gifter; no crest ever enters Subscriber.
- **Frame = provenance.** Merit: plain ring. Team: **keyed system-seal rim** (chrome only the platform renders → unfakeable). Subscriber: a **tenure-arc** that fills like a charge meter. Gifter: a **soft radiating warm aura**.
- **State = standing vs permanence.** Merit + Team are **permanent** (matte/struck/"forever"). Subscriber + Gifter are **living** (glow/charge/pulse) and visibly **DIM to dormant when payment lapses** — so paid status never looks like a permanently-earned trophy.
- **Motion only at each family's apex** (Founder shimmer, Lifetime crystal pulse, Guardian corona breath). Everything below static.
- **Prestige order in the row:** Team (authority, locked to name) › Merit (earned) › Subscriber (standing) › Gifter (rendered slightly softer so a *bought* coin can never visually outrank an earned or staff one).

## ★ SHOWCASE MECHANICS (how they drive desire)

- **Trophy Case grid** — full catalog on the profile; earned = full metal, unearned = greyed frosted silhouettes with the locked frame visible. The visible GAP ("9 of 14 collected") is the completion engine + tells users what to chase.
- **3 Featured slots only** — limited display next to your name/avatar; you curate your best three (scarcity of display is itself status). System orders them by rarity.
- **Live rarity popover** — tap a badge → name, tier, **earned-% ("held by 3.4%")**, and a pre-credited progress bar to the next tier.
- **Rarest-badge showcase** (Steam-style) — an auto slot that always shows your rarest badge + its %, so the system brags for you.
- **Feed unlock moments** — unlocking a notable/secret/seasonal badge surfaces a premium animated reveal card in the feed = soft recommendation + referral driver.
- **Segmented leaderboards** (never global) — by zone/council/season/friend-pool (~30, reset Mondays), framed relatively ("top 8%") so most users hit a "top" moment.
- **Blackout meta-flex** — Pro/prestige can toggle the whole set to monochrome platinum.
- **Badges unlock real estate** — extra slots, Arpubes themes, feed controls, deeper AXON allowances. Collecting upgrades the profile materially.

## ★ SCARCITY LEVERS (build the permanent ones NOW)

- **Founder hard cutoff** — minted once, never re-issued; rarity only grows. *You can only ever grant "earliest believer" once in the platform's life — set the cutoff now.*
- **Closed early-cohort window (OG)** — behavior-gated, permanently closes (Discord Early Supporter model).
- **Seasonal expiry** (Season Vanguard, Council) — hard deadlines, unique dated art, supply capped when the window shuts → old seasons become relics.
- **Secret/hidden** (Phantom Hour) — undiscoverable, twin-detected; the most-shared badges.
- **Program-gated elite tiers** (Course Creator Prestige, EPHAS Team) — reachable on-ramp, near-mythical ceiling (Bug Hunter Silver/Gold).
- **Grandfather, never erase** — when you sunset a feature, convert its marker into a permanent "Legacy/Alumni" badge. (Discord deleting Active Developer angered users — always grandfather.)
- **Losable status** (streaks, Zone Sovereign) — decay keeps holding it a live scarcity signal.
- **Visible earned-%** on every badge — AXON's own analytics prove the scarcity; a 0.3% badge is self-evidently aspirational.
