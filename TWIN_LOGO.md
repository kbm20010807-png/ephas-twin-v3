# TWIN — Logo concepts (from deep discovery)

Brand: gold + platinum on dark. A symbol only (no letters), simple, iconic, unique, crisp at 16px.
Meaning to embody: the split between your current self and your better/rising self.

Reference sketches: `_cleave_concepts.png` (all three), `static/twin_cleave.png` (CLEAVE).

---

## 1. CLEAVE  ⭐ (recommended)
**Meaning:** one self, split at the moment of choice — the lifted gold half is the future you, already a half-step ahead.

**AI prompt (ChatGPT / Gemini):**
> Minimalist flat vector logo: a single bold upward chevron/caret split down its center into two halves, the right half lifted a half-step higher than the left so a thin diagonal seam of empty space shears between them. Left half in cool platinum silver, right half in warm gold, both clean flat gradients. Sharp mitered apex, butt-cap stroke ends, thick confident strokes, NOT a filled triangle. Solid dark charcoal background, no chrome, no bevel, no drop shadow, no text. Modern, geometric, premium, symbolic. Designed as a scalable app icon, centered, generous padding. Transparent PNG, flat design, high contrast, crisp at small sizes.

---

## 2. ECHO ASCENDING  (elegant runner-up)
**Meaning:** your future self is your own shape, just further along — the lighter echo is the you that's already arrived.

**AI prompt:**
> Minimalist flat vector logo: a single bold diagonal stroke rising upward to the right, with an identical lighter copy of itself offset up and forward like a head-start shadow. Two parallel strokes, never crossing, the lower one solid warm gold, the upper one pale translucent platinum so it reads as a future echo. Clean cut ends, equal weight, equal angle. Solid dark charcoal background, no text, no chrome, no bevel, no drop shadow. Modern, geometric, premium, symbolic of momentum and a future self. Designed as a scalable app icon, centered, generous padding. Transparent PNG, flat design, high contrast, crisp at small sizes.

---

## 3. THE GAP  (clever negative-space)
**Meaning:** two pillars you see; the ascent hidden between them — the reward you notice on the second look.

**AI prompt:**
> Minimalist flat vector logo: two thick vertical rounded bars of unequal height standing as a rising pair tilted slightly upward to the right, the negative space between their angled inner edges forming a clean hidden upward-pointing arrow. Bars in warm gold flat gradient on a solid dark charcoal background. The arrow is empty space, revealed between the bars, bold and unmistakable. No text, no chrome, no bevel, no drop shadow. Modern, geometric, premium, clever negative-space mark. Designed as a scalable app icon, centered, generous padding. Transparent PNG, flat design, high contrast, crisp at small sizes.
