## 1. Our Community Values

TWIN exists to help you become a better version of yourself. Your AI coach, AXON, fuses your real data — your health and wearables, your finances, your voice, your food, your workouts, your habits — into a living digital twin that helps you grow. That only works if TWIN is a place you can trust.

Trust is fragile, and the data here is personal. So we ask everyone to follow a simple idea:

**Be real. Be kind. Be safe. Help each other grow.**

These Guidelines explain what that means in practice — what's welcome, what's not allowed, how we enforce the rules, and how to report a problem or appeal a decision. They apply everywhere on TWIN: your posts, comments, direct messages, profile, badges, courses, creator content, and how you interact with AXON.

By using TWIN, you agree to follow these Guidelines alongside our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. We wrote them in plain language on purpose. If something here is unclear, that's on us — reach out and we'll explain.

A quick note on our design choices, because they shape these rules:

- **Your sensitive data stays on your device where it can.** Voice-emotion analysis and workout-video analysis happen on your device. We don't build voiceprints. We don't sell your data. We don't run advertising SDKs against your sensitive health or financial data.
- **TWIN supports wellness, not medicine.** AXON is a coach, not a doctor, therapist, or financial adviser.
- **Finance is read-only.** TWIN reads your financial data to help you understand it. It never moves your money.
- **You must be 16 or older to use TWIN.**

## 2. What's Not Allowed

Our rules are grouped under four pillars: **Safety**, **Respect**, **Authenticity**, and **Responsible Wellness**. Each rule below follows the same pattern: the rule, what it includes, and any exceptions.

Where you see content about self-harm, disordered eating, or crisis, our first response is support — we surface help and resources, not just penalties.

---

### PILLAR A — SAFETY

Keeping people safe comes before everything else. Some rules in this pillar have zero tolerance, meaning we act immediately and without warning.

#### A1. Violence, Threats, and Incitement
**The rule:** Don't threaten, glorify, incite, or organize violence against people.

*This includes:* credible threats of harm; celebrating or promoting violent acts or perpetrators; calls for others to commit violence; content from or praising violent extremist or terrorist organizations.

*Exceptions:* clearly educational, journalistic, condemning, or fictional context — as long as it isn't a genuine threat or a call to action.

#### A2. Suicide and Self-Harm
**The rule:** Don't promote, encourage, provide instructions for, or normalize suicide or self-injury.

*This includes:* how-to methods, self-harm challenges, content that pressures others toward self-harm, or glorification of these acts.

*Our approach:* If you're struggling, you are not in trouble. When our systems or a report detect a user in crisis, TWIN and AXON respond with crisis resources and support first. Recovery stories, seeking help, and honest conversations about mental health are welcome — we distinguish sharing a struggle from promoting harm. Content that crosses into promotion or instruction is removed, and we may pair removal with a check-in and resources.

#### A3. Eating Disorders and Disordered Eating
**The rule:** Don't promote, glorify, or provide instructions for disordered eating.

*This includes:* pro-anorexia or pro-bulimia content; extreme calorie restriction as a goal to imitate; purging techniques; "thinspiration"; crash-diet or fasting-streak challenges framed as achievements.

*Our approach:* Because TWIN tracks food, body, and fitness, this is a heightened-risk area for us. Searches related to eating disorders surface support resources. Recovery-focused and clinically responsible content is allowed. See also **D1** and **D3** under Responsible Wellness.

#### A4. Dangerous Acts and Challenges
**The rule:** Don't post dangerous stunts, viral challenges, or acts that risk serious injury or death.

*This includes:* choking or blackout games; extreme fasting, dehydration, or over-exercise challenges; unsafe "transformation" stunts; dangerous use of substances or equipment.

*Exceptions:* professional or clearly supervised feats with appropriate context and warnings, where imitation isn't encouraged.

#### A5. Child Safety (Zero Tolerance)
**The rule:** We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

*This includes:* any sexual content involving minors, real or generated; sexualization of minors; grooming; and any attempt to solicit or share such material.

*Our approach:* This content is removed immediately, the account is permanently banned with no warning, and we report to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and relevant authorities as required by law. TWIN is a 16+ service; accounts we believe belong to someone under 16 are removed.

#### A6. Regulated and Illegal Goods
**The rule:** Don't sell, trade, or promote regulated or illegal goods and services.

*This includes:* illegal drugs and controlled substances; weapons and explosives; and — given our context — unregulated or unsafe supplements, "miracle" health products, steroids/SARMs, unproven medical treatments, and fraudulent financial products or schemes.

*Exceptions:* general educational discussion (for example, harm-reduction or a licensed creator explaining a topic) that doesn't facilitate a sale.

### PILLAR B — RESPECT

TWIN is about growth, and people grow faster where they feel safe and respected.

#### B1. Hate and Dehumanization
**The rule:** Don't attack, dehumanize, or call for the exclusion of people based on who they are.

*Protected attributes:* race, ethnicity, national origin, caste, religion, sex, gender identity, sexual orientation, age, disability, or serious disease.

*This includes:* slurs, hateful stereotypes, dehumanizing comparisons, denial of well-documented atrocities, and hateful symbols used to attack.

*Exceptions:* quoting or reporting on hate to condemn or educate, when clearly framed that way.

#### B2. Harassment and Bullying
**The rule:** Don't target people with harassment, degradation, or unwanted contact.

*This includes:* targeted insults; coordinated pile-ons; repeated unwanted messages after someone asks you to stop; body-shaming; mocking someone's health, weight, income, or financial situation; and sexual harassment or unwanted sexual advances.

*Exceptions:* good-faith criticism of public figures, ideas, or public content — the line is between critiquing what someone does and attacking who they are.

#### B3. Nudity and Sexual Content
**The rule:** Keep TWIN non-pornographic. Don't post sexually explicit content or solicit sexual services.

*This includes:* pornography; explicit sexual acts; sexual solicitation; and — with zero tolerance — non-consensual intimate imagery ("revenge porn") and threats to share it.

*Exceptions:* health, fitness, medical, breastfeeding, or educational contexts that aren't sexualized. Non-consensual intimate imagery has no exception and triggers an immediate ban.

### PILLAR C — AUTHENTICITY

Your digital twin is only meaningful if it's genuinely yours. Fakery corrodes the entire community.

#### C1. Be Authentic — No Fake Accounts or Manipulation
**The rule:** Be a real person being real. Don't manipulate the platform.

*This includes:* bots and fake accounts; artificial follows, likes, streaks, or badge activity; fabricated progress or metrics designed to deceive others; and coordinated inauthentic behavior.

*Exceptions:* clearly labeled parody, fan, or commentary accounts that don't impersonate or deceive.

#### C2. No Impersonation
**The rule:** Don't pretend to be someone or something you're not in order to deceive.

*This includes:* impersonating another person, a creator, a brand, TWIN staff, or **AXON itself**. Don't present your own words as if they came from the AI coach.

*Exceptions:* clearly marked parody or fan accounts.

#### C3. No Spam or Scams
**The rule:** Don't spam, and don't run scams.

*This includes:* mass unsolicited messages or links; repetitive low-value posting; phishing; pyramid, Ponzi, and "get-rich-quick" schemes; fake giveaways; and deceptive affiliate or referral spam. Financial scams are treated as high-severity given our audience.

#### C4. Misinformation and AI Transparency
**The rule:** Don't spread false information that can cause real harm, and be honest about AI-generated content.

*This includes:* medical misinformation that could lead to physical harm; dangerous financial misinformation; media manipulated to deceive; and AI-generated images, audio, or text presented as authentic without a label.

*Our approach:* We focus enforcement on misinformation with a real-world harm risk — especially health and money, which are the heart of TWIN. Clearly label AI-generated content, and don't present AXON's output as verified professional advice (see **D5**).

### PILLAR D — RESPONSIBLE WELLNESS (TWIN-Specific)

TWIN sits at the intersection of health, money, and AI. That's powerful, and it carries responsibility. These rules exist because our core loop — tracking your body, your habits, and your finances — can be genuinely helpful, but can also attract pressure toward disordered eating, over-exercise, body-image spirals, and risky money behavior. We take extra care here.

Throughout this pillar, enforcement is supportive first: we pair removals with resources, not just penalties.

#### D1. No Medical or Health Claims
**The rule:** TWIN and AXON support wellness — they don't practice medicine.

*This includes not:* diagnosing, treating, or claiming to cure conditions; guaranteeing health outcomes; presenting content or AXON responses as a substitute for a licensed medical or mental-health professional; or promoting extreme diets, prolonged fasting, unverified supplements, or unproven "protocols" as safe or effective.

*What's welcome:* sharing your own journey, general wellness education, and encouragement — framed as personal experience, not medical instruction. **AXON provides general wellness information, not medical advice. Always consult a qualified professional for medical decisions.**

#### D2. No Financial Advice
**The rule:** TWIN reads your finances to help you understand them. It does not give personalized financial advice, and neither should the community.

*This includes not:* offering personalized investment, tax, or legal advice; promising guaranteed returns; running "get-rich-quick" content; pumping specific stocks or crypto; or pushing high-risk financial "challenges."

*What's welcome:* general financial education, budgeting tips, and sharing your own experience — framed as information, not a recommendation. **TWIN's finance features are read-only and never move your money. AXON provides general educational information, not financial advice. Consult a licensed professional for financial decisions.**

#### D3. No Harmful Body-Image or Comparison Content
**The rule:** Don't promote unhealthy targets or shame people's bodies, weight, or money.

*This includes:* content promoting disordered eating or over-exercise (see **A3**, **A4**); body-shaming or fat-shaming; setting extreme weight, body-fat, or financial targets as goals for others to copy; and toxic comparison content designed to make people feel inadequate about their body or finances.

*Our approach:* We aim to keep TWIN a place of healthy motivation, not corrosive comparison. Progress-sharing and honest before/afters are fine when they don't promote extremes.

#### D4. Protecting Vulnerable Users
**The rule:** We build guardrails for people who may be at risk, and we ask the community to do the same.

*This includes:* not encouraging anyone toward self-harm, disordered eating, over-exercise, or financial risk; not targeting or exploiting people who are visibly struggling. Searches and reports tied to self-harm or eating disorders surface crisis resources. AXON is designed to recognize signs of distress and respond supportively, de-escalate, and point toward help — never to push a vulnerable user harder.

#### D5. AI Transparency and Safe Use of AXON
**The rule:** Be clear about AI, and don't misuse the coach.

*This includes:* clearly labeling AI-generated content you post; not presenting AXON's output as professional medical, financial, or legal advice; not impersonating AXON; and not trying to jailbreak or manipulate AXON into producing prohibited content (self-harm instructions, medical/financial advice presented as professional, hateful content, etc.).

*Reminder:* AXON is a supportive coach built on your data. It can be wrong. For anything that affects your health, money, or legal situation, talk to a qualified professional.

### Privacy and Other People's Data

Because TWIN handles unusually sensitive information, we call this out on its own.

**The rule:** Don't share other people's private information without their consent.

*This includes:* doxxing (posting someone's address, contact details, location, or identity documents); and — specific to TWIN — sharing another person's **health data, financial data, voice recordings, or biometric information** without their clear consent. Don't post someone else's medical results, bank details, or body/weight data. Don't upload another person's voice or workout video to analyze them without permission.

*Exceptions:* information the subject has clearly made public themselves, shared in a non-harmful way.

## 3. How We Enforce

We aim to be fair, proportionate, and human about enforcement. What we do depends on **three things: the severity of the violation, your history on TWIN, and which category was involved.**

### First violation = a warning (for low-severity issues)
For most minor, first-time violations, you'll get a **warning and an explanation**, not a penalty. We'd rather help you understand the rule than punish you.

### The strike system
For repeat or more serious violations, we use a graduated strike system with a **90-day rolling window** (strikes older than 90 days no longer count toward escalation):

- **Warning** — notice, content removed if needed, no penalty.
- **Strike 1** — temporary limits (for example, a short posting freeze).
- **Strike 2 (within 90 days)** — a longer posting freeze and reduced reach.
- **Strike 3 (within 90 days)** — extended suspension or permanent removal.

Repeat strikes **in the same category** escalate faster.

### Actions short of a ban
Enforcement isn't all-or-nothing. Depending on the situation we may:

- remove or restrict the content,
- reduce its distribution (not recommending it in the feed),
- limit features (commenting, messaging, livestreaming, going viral),
- age-gate content,
- apply guardrails to AXON for the account,
- temporarily suspend the account.

### Severe violations = immediate permanent ban (no warning)
Some violations skip the ladder entirely. We remove the content and permanently ban the account with no warning for:

- child sexual abuse or exploitation (**A5**),
- credible threats of serious violence, and violent extremist content (**A1**),
- non-consensual intimate imagery (**B3**),
- actively encouraging or instructing suicide or self-harm (**A2**),
- serious, repeated, or coordinated attempts to harm others.

We also reserve the right to remove content or accounts that violate the spirit of these Guidelines or our Terms, and to report unlawful activity to authorities.

## 4. Reporting and Appeals

### Reporting
Every post, comment, profile, direct message, course, and creator has a **Report** button. Tap it, choose a category, and send. Reports are confidential. In an emergency, contact your local emergency services first.

If you report self-harm or someone in crisis, TWIN will surface **crisis resources** alongside your report — our first priority is getting people help.

### What happens next
When we take action against your content or account, we'll tell you **which rule was broken** and what action we took. If we remove something in error, we'll restore it.

### Appeals
If you think we got it wrong, you can appeal. You have **6 months** from the decision to submit an appeal through the in-app form or the link in your notice. We commit to reviewing appeals in a **timely, good-faith, non-discriminatory** way, and to restoring content that was removed in error. (We may decline to reinstate content only where there's a serious ongoing safety risk.)

### Transparency
As TWIN grows, we intend to publish periodic transparency reports summarizing reports received, actions taken, and appeals resolved.

### Contact
- Report in-app: **Report** on any content or profile.
- Safety or privacy concerns: **[safety@twin.app / trust@twin.app]**
- Appeals: in-app **Appeal** form.

Thank you for helping keep TWIN real, kind, safe, and growth-focused.

## NOTES FOR YOUR LAWYER

*This section is internal guidance for legal review — remove before publishing.*

1. **Cross-reference, don't duplicate.** These Guidelines must sit alongside a Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, EULA, and creator/subscription agreements. Confirm the ToS incorporates these Guidelines by reference and that precedence between documents is defined. Medical/financial disclaimers here are community-facing summaries — the binding disclaimers and limitation-of-liability language belong in the ToS.

2. **Medical and financial disclaimers need teeth.** The "no medical advice / no financial advice" language (D1, D2, D5) is liability-critical for an AI-coach app. Have counsel draft robust standalone disclaimers, "not a medical device" positioning (confirm TWIN stays outside FDA / EU MDR / Qatar MoPH medical-device classification for AXON), and clear limitation-of-liability + indemnification clauses. Confirm AXON's wellness-not-diagnosis framing holds up in every launch market, especially Gulf/Qatar.

3. **Strictest-common-denominator compliance.** Verify these Guidelines and the underlying data flows satisfy: **GDPR** (lawful basis, data-subject rights, DPIA for sensitive/health data, appeals as required by the **EU Digital Services Act** — note DSA also triggers transparency-reporting and notice-and-appeal obligations at scale), **BIPA** (Illinois — voice-emotion: confirm on-device processing with no voiceprint keeps you outside "biometric identifier"; still consider written consent + retention/destruction policy), **MHMDA** (Washington My Health My Data — broad "consumer health data" definition likely covers wearables, food, workout, mood; needs a separate consent + a Consumer Health Data Privacy Policy), **CCPA/CPRA** (sensitive personal information category, opt-out, no-sale confirmation), and **PIPL** (China — cross-border transfer and separate consent; assess whether you serve/store any China data at all).

4. **Age of 16+.** Confirm 16 as the floor works across markets. GDPR digital-consent age varies by member state (13–16); COPPA (US) is under 13; some Gulf jurisdictions have their own minor-protection rules. Decide whether 16 is a hard global floor or region-adjusted, and define age-assurance methods and what happens to under-16 accounts and their data.

5. **Financial data = regulated territory.** "Read-only finance" via aggregation likely involves an Open Banking / data-aggregation provider (e.g., Plaid-type) and possibly Qatar Central Bank / QFC rules and PSD2-style obligations in the EU. Confirm licensing posture, that "read-only" is contractually and technically enforced, and that the "no financial advice" stance keeps TWIN outside investment-adviser / robo-adviser regulation in each market.

6. **CSAE reporting obligations.** A5 commits to NCMEC reporting — confirm the legal reporting pathway and retention/preservation obligations in each jurisdiction (NCMEC is US-specific; Gulf and EU have their own mandated channels). Ensure a documented CSAE detection-and-report process exists before launch.

7. **Health/crisis liability.** The crisis-resource routing (A2, A3, D4) is good practice but creates a "duty" narrative — have counsel advise on Good Samaritan positioning, jurisdiction-appropriate hotlines (Qatar vs. global), and language that avoids implying TWIN provides clinical intervention or monitoring.

8. **AXON output liability.** Because AXON generates advice-adjacent content from personal data, address: who is liable for AI errors, whether outputs are logged, and how the "jailbreak" prohibition (D5) interacts with your AI safety controls and any AI-specific regulation (EU AI Act — assess risk-classification; likely limited/minimal-risk, but health-adjacent AI can draw scrutiny).

9. **Content moderation staffing reality.** As a solo founder, confirm you can actually deliver on the enforcement, review, and 6-month appeal commitments made here. Under-delivering on published commitments is itself a legal/consumer-protection risk (and a DSA risk at scale). Consider softening firm timelines to "we aim to" where you can't yet guarantee SLAs, and document your moderation process.

10. **Governing law, dispute resolution, and localization.** Set governing law, venue, and dispute-resolution (arbitration?) in the ToS. Given the Gulf/Qatar home market, confirm alignment with Qatari law and any Arabic-language requirement for consumer-facing terms; plan localized versions of these Guidelines.
